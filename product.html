Food Safety Statement

To deliver the highest standards of product,
conduct of business, and service to you.

To ensure the safety and quality of fish
and processed goods intended for domestic
and international markets through modern
facilities and infrastructures of General
Santos Fish Port Complex located at Makar,
General Santos City, as well as by technical
soundness and truthfulness of product
information for the protection of consumers.

To maintain a healthy pond environment,
because it’s easier and cheaper to
manage a safe environment. Fish grow
better, reproduce more effectively
and have a higher survival rate if
the water quality is good and the
pond is not overcrowded. Also, a
flow-through water system is best
recommended in keeping a clean and
quality water.

About Us

of our branding. We want our products to be
considered the “gold standard” of seafoods
and fresh water products. Our wholesale, retail
and fingerlings customers all expect quality
and service throughout our entire operation.

logo symbolizes the 3 major areas in our company –
Wholesale, Retail & Our Fingerlings.
